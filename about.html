About Us

MD Humanitarian Foundation[...Mission for Development] is a non-governmental organization dedicated in fostering the holistic development of children and youth while actively engaging in humanitarian efforts to create social impact . Our programs focus on providing education, healthcare, and social support to children and youth, empowering them to reach their full potential. Additionally, we work on initiatives that promote community development, advocacy for children's rights, and humanitarian aid to vulnerable populations, contributing to a more inclusive and compassionate society.
